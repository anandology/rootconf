Rootconf is an event with 2 days of workshops followed by 2 days of conference on web and cloud infrastructure. It will help you to plan and develop a strategy map for infrastructure and devops and will show you the building blocks for reaching a strategy for Continous Integration, Deployment and Delivery. It is targeted at companies who are struggling to scale the effectiveness of their developer teams and performance of their web stacks.

The first edition of Rootconf was held on May 2012, at Dharamaram Vidya Kshethram, Bangalore. The single day three track conference featured Configuration Deployment/Management, Performance Tuning, Security, Virtualization, Cloud & Monitoring.
