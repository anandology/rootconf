What is Rootconf?

Rootconf is India's principal conference where systems and operations engineers share real world knowledge about building resilient and scalable systems. This year’s theme is infrastructure automation. The conference will feature talks on predictive orchestration, cloud and data center automation, and elements critical to web scalability and high availability. There will also be chatter about monitoring patterns.

Why you should attend Rootconf?

If you’re a part of DevOps at your company, are a CTO / tech-lead, budding entrepreneur looking to get a better grip on tech or simply a tech enthusiast looking to meet like minded people, then the answer is YES! RootConf 2015 brings together some of the best minds in this industry, and you sure don’t want to miss it.

What will you learn?

The 2015 edition of RootConf will feature talks on a host of topics ranging from ‘Introduction to SaltStack’ to a ‘Quick Primer on HTTP2’ and ‘Why favour Icinga over Nagios’ to ‘Why Prefer PostgreSQL to MySQL’, to name a few. So, if you’re looking to scale the effectiveness of your developer team, enhance performance of your web stacks or even develop a continuous integration and deployment strategy, RootConf 2015 will serve as the perfect learning ground for you.
