Rootconf is a conference on web and cloud infrastructure. It is targeted at companies who are struggling to scale the effectiveness of their developer teams and performance of their web stacks.

Developing a continuous integration, deployment and delivery strategy is critical for the cadence of the team. Infrastructure and DevOps is an upfront investment (human, time, money) process. The challenge always is whether you're willing to make that investment right away, or in the future at a much higher cost and effort.

Rootconf is a conference which will help you to plan and develop a strategy map for infrastructure and devops. It will show you the building blocks for reaching a strategy for Continous Integration, Deployment and Delivery.
